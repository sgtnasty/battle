Battle Engine

Definitions
-----------
Adventure
	A series of encounters.
Encounter
	An event where PC's are tested (combat, stealth, persuasion, etc)

example adventure pack:
<adventure name="Ruins on the borderlands">
	<intro>Long text here describing the players intro.</intro>
	<encounters>
		<encounter name="Mysterious Pass">
			<task name="Deafeat monsters in pass">
			</task>
		</encounter>
	</encounters>
</adventure>
